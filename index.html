Crystals Collector


You will be given a random number.

There are four crystals below. Clicking one will add a specfic amount to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number. You lose by having your score go over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden.

At the start of each new game the value of each crystal will change.
